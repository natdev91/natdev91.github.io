Privacy Policy

Super Simple Workout Timer · Natawill Industries · Last updated: June 19, 2026

Super Simple Workout Timer is a local interval timer. It does not require an account, does not connect to any server, and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data we collect

None. The app has no analytics, no crash reporting, no login, and no user-generated content. All timer settings are stored only on your device and are never sent anywhere.

Advertising

The app displays banner ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information to serve and measure ads, including your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and general location. This data collection is governed by Google's own privacy policy, which you can review at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalised advertising on iOS by going to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising and turning off Personalised Ads.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the date at the top of this page. Given the app collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
